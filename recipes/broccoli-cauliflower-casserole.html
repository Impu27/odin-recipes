Description

Broccoli Cauliflower Casserole is a comforting and hearty dish that combines two nutritious vegetables—broccoli and cauliflower—into a creamy, baked casserole. The vegetables are typically blanched or steamed to tenderize them before being mixed with a creamy sauce made from ingredients like cream cheese, sour cream, or a creamy soup base. Cheddar cheese, garlic, and seasonings are often added to enhance flavor. The casserole is then topped with a layer of shredded cheese or breadcrumbs for a crispy, golden finish. It is a popular side dish for holidays or family dinners, offering a rich and savory way to enjoy vegetables while adding a satisfying texture contrast.

Ingredients

  • Broccoli florets - 1 cup
  • Cauliflower florets - 1 cup
  • Onion - 1
  • Garlic - 3 cloves
  • Cream cheese - 1/2 cup
  • Sour cream - 1 cup
  • Cheddar cheese - 1 cup
  • Milk - 2 cups
  • Butter - 1 cube
  • Crispy fried onions
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cook vegetables: Steam or blanch broccoli and cauliflower until tender, then drain well.
  3. Prepare sauce: In a bowl, mix softened cream cheese, sour cream, garlic, shredded cheddar cheese, salt, and pepper. Optionally, add milk to adjust the sauce consistency.
  4. Combine: Toss the cooked broccoli and cauliflower with the creamy sauce, ensuring they're well-coated.
  5. Assemble casserole: Transfer the mixture to a greased baking dish, spreading it evenly.
  6. Top: Sprinkle with extra cheddar cheese and breadcrumbs (or crispy fried onions) if desired.
  7. Bake: Place in the oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until bubbly and golden on top.
